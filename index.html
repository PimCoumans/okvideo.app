Tap-to-record video camera limited only by your creativity

OK Video is unlimited, vine-style video recording without any editing. Easily create a vlog, a stop motion or just record whatever’s happening. After recording you can directly save it to your camera roll or send it to your favorite app. If you do need editing, like removing mistakes or reordering shots, the Timeline Editor is there to help!

Your OK Videos are exported with a small watermark at the bottom. You can remove this watermark with a single in-app purchase found in the settings menu.
