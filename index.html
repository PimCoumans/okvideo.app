OK Video

free tap-to-record camera

Tap-to-record video app limited only by your creativity. No annoying ads, no hidden limits, does exactly what it says on the tin.

OK Video is unlimited, vine-style video recording with no editing needed: simply hold the screen to record each shot of your video. Easily create a vlog, a stop motion video or just capture whatever’s happening around you. After recording you can directly save it to your camera roll or share it via your favorite app. If you do need a little editing — like removing mistakes or reordering shots — the Timeline Editor is there to help! (for a one-time in-app purchase)
